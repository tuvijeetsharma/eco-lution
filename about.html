Who are we?

Derived from (Eco)logy and Evo(lution); Eco-Lution is a venture aimed at finding reliable, affordable and impactful solutions to environmental issues all over the world.

Interested? Read on.

The problem we seek to solve.

More than 500 billion disposable plastic cups are being used every year, and being thrown away immediately after use. Around a million people die every year because of diseases and accidents linked to plastic wastes. That's pretty much a death every 30 seconds. Plastics take more than 3000 years to decompose, so they’re also a major source of pollution. In plastic cups for instance, there are more than millions of microplastic particles. When we drink, these plastics and chemicals involved in production seep into our beverage, our body, and poison us slowly. We’re desperately in need of fresh material - and here’s where our product comes in.

The opportunity that presents itself.

Our solution - the Eco-lution cups are completely handmade, eco-friendly, biodegradable, and non-beverage-polluting! There’s nothing like them in the market, you can choose to reuse them and when you dispose of them they’ll just go straight back to the soil! The most intriguing feature is that they even have a dormant seed in the bottom, ready to germinate, so you get a bonus plant, and you're also contributing to the environment.
